Our Workflow Roadmap

From discovery to reliable, monitored production systems

Discovery

Align on business goals, success metrics, constraints, and data availability.

Data Ingestion

Ingest from APIs, DBs, files, events. Validate schemas & quality at the edge.

Exploration

EDA, profiling, drift checks, bias scans; define baselines & candidate features.

Features & Modeling

Feature store integration; classical ML & deep learning; prompt/LLM design.

Evaluation

Deployment

Monitoring & Iteration

Telemetry, drift & quality monitoring, feedback loops, continuous improvement.
